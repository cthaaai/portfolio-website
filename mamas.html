User Surveys

A great way to kick start research for any projecy, surveys are quick, inexpensive and provide a useful basis for scripting interview questions. The survey included 12 questions and involved 30 participants. The research provided a whole slew of quantitative data which painted a more complete picture of the needs, goals, pain points and opinions of my users.

User Interviews

A simple way to gain honest and direct feedback from a target audience. The insight gained help to understand users goals, needs, emotions and behaviours. Face-to-face interviews allow extra attention to their tone of voice and body language as well as allows for follow-up questions which aid in a more complete picture of user's thoughts and feelings. My user interviews consisted of 12 questions, among 5 respondents which took approximately 45 minutes each to conduct.
